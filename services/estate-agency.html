Estate Agency

We engage in the marketing of developed and undeveloped properties by seeking purchase, sale, letting of such properties as duplexes, detached houses, block of flats, plots etc.

We also offer professional advice on sale, purchases and letting on properties. In carrying out this function, we generally advertise the property to let or to be sold. The many ways of advertising the properties for letting or sales includes:

  • Advertising the property in the National dailies newspaper, Property Magazines, flyers And Handbills
  • Advertising the Property by placing a TO LET/LEASE OR FOR SALE board on the said Property
  • Advertising the Property by introducing it to Prospective Clients, Individuals, Organizations and Professional Colleagues through mails refers to as ‘OFFER LETTER’.
  • Advertising the Property on Radio and Television in some cases
  • Midwife negotiations between either landlord and the prospective tenant/buyer and seller of the interest in land
  • Document transactions such as preparation of lease agreement, power of attorney, deed of assignment and memorandum of understanding.
  • Handover the property to tenant/buyer/assignee/mortgagor etc.
