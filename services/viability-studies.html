DEVELOPMENT FEASIBILITY AND VIABILITY STUDIES

To achieve the optimum level of returns from an investment, it is desirable that a feasibility and viability study is carried out so as to identify its practicability and whether it is worth-while. We offer Consultancy services on suitability of property Investment in which we advise on highest and best use for property by carrying out feasibility and viability tests whereby we draw up projections on return on Investment (R.O.I). We investigate titles and carry out Market Surveys and advice accordingly. We undertake analysis of the entire frame of a proposed project covering the following areas:

  • Project definition, scope and description
  • Analysis of Physical, Legal and Political attribute of the proposed project.
  • Analysis of the investment cost and financing plans.
  • Market analysis of the completed.
