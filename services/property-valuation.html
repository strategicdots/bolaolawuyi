Independent property valuation

Valuation is the analytical and scientific process of ascending the market worth of interests in property assets at particular point in time and for a specific purpose. Such property assets include real estate, plants and machinery, Motor Vehicles, Furniture, Trade Stocks, Livestock and the like. The purpose which we undertake valuations are follows:

  • Sale
  • Purchase
  • Compulsory acquisition and
  • compensation
  • Property Rating
  • Mergers, Takeover and splitting up of companies
  • Auditing, Balance Sheet/Preparation of Statement of Affairs
  • Mortgage
  • Offer of Shares to the public
  • Insurance. Etc.

The other types of valuation we engage in include but not limited to:

  • Valuation for construction projects
  • Valuation for oil and gas
  • Valuation of aviation and navigation installations, including aircrafts, ships, and vessels.
  • Environmental impact survey, analysis and valuation
