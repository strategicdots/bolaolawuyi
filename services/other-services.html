PROPERTY AND FACILITIES MANAGEMENT

This encompasses the general administration of various interest held in landed properties on behalf of the owners with a view to maintaining the property values throughout the period of ownership and avoiding premature obsolescence of properties through property utilization, rehabilitation and management studies.

We equally manage the owner's services and the service charge accounting in a multi-tenanted accommodation. We administer service provision on properties we manage. Such services include those for water supply, electricity supply, security, cleaning of common areas etc.

we create a separate account for the administration of these services. First we determine services to be provided per annum from which we determine amount and time of payment.

CONSTRUCTION/PROJECT MANAGEMENT

This is the coordination of construction activities on site, curtailing wastes and ensuring completion of project on schedule within the define scope, quality, time and cost constraints.

We at Bola Olawuyi Consulting consider that every development process must be tailored towards specific objective, thus, it is imperative that the Estate Surveyor and Valuer is best equipped to select and co-ordinate other professionals (as project Managers) for effective implementation of the project plan.
