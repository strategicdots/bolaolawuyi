OLAWUYI KUBURAT BOLA (ANIVS, RSV, MNIM)

Head of practice

Olawuyi kuburat Bola holds Higher National Diploma in Estate Management from Kwara State Polytechnic, Ilorin, Kwara State. She is an Associate Member of the Nigerian Institution of Estate Surveyors and Valuers (NIESV) and a Registered Member of the profession with Estate Surveyors and Valuers Registration Board (ESVARBON).

She is a member of Nigeria Institute of Management. She has worked with Ministry of housing, reputable firms of Estate Surveyors and Valuers, Property Company such as Lagos State ministry of housing, Messrs Adefila, Akinsomi Salako and partners, Messrs Niyi Fadoju and Associates.

she is also a director at KBO Properties Limited before setting up the practice. She constantly attends seminars, conferences and continuing professional development programmes to improve her professional knowledge and ability to deliver high standard services to her clients.

She is a core professional who believes in ethical practice.She has acquired knowledge and experience in waste management, climate change/global warming, oil and gas economics, acquisition and compensation, environmental management.

She has successfully participated in courses such as Land Administration & Management: The FCT Experience, The New National Housing Policy, The financial reporting council of Nigeria Act, 2011 and Estate Surveying and Valuation Practice in Nigeria, opportunities, challenges and responsibilities and Valuation in the oil and gas industry (International standard and methodology) to mention a few. She has 18yrs working experience.
